Use the middle and one of the outer legs of the potentiometer when connecting your RC circuit.

Pay attention to the circuit diagram - the location of ground is very important. (The black alligator clip is grounded.)

Get to know the Function Generator!  
Ensure the TTL symbol is activated by pressing the SHIFT button followed by the WAVE button!
Activate the TTL output using OUTPUT ON button.  LED green indicator should be visible.
