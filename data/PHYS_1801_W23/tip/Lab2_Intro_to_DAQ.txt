Use the breadboard and provided wires to connect to the potentiometer/motor to the Power Supply and to the SensorDAQ.

DO NOT REMOVE THE POTENTIOMETER FROM THE BREADBOARD.

SensorDAQ terminal description:
6 -> +5V output
5,8 or 10 -> Ground (GND)
9 -> Analog Output 0 (AO0)
11 -> Analog Input 0 (AI0)
