Login to Laptop using your passport York account only.

Check if instrument rack USB-HUB cable is not connected to laptop at the beginner of the LAB.
Connect this cable to laptop before using Wavefor_ver2.vi and or SCOPE TO DESKTOP applications.
In SCOPE TO DESKTOP application check "VISA resource name" (left bottom corner) and change SCOPE-25 to SCOPE-N,
where N is your laptop number (use menu arrow).

Logout from your account at the end of Lab and disconnect instrument USB-HUB cable from your laptop.
